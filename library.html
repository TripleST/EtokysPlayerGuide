The Spelljammer stories are a collection of writings from our D&D campaign, set in a custom Spelljammer setting. If you aren't familiar with Spelljammer as a concept, think of it like a fantasy pirate adventure, but in space! (Or like Treasure Planet with magic). Our crew of adventurers set off to uncover the mysteries of the galaxy on our trusty Spelljammer (a magic ship) and stop the Clockwork Horrors from their plans to control all life and magic itself.

The character I played, Aya, is an Artificer (a magic-user who specializes in magic items), as well as an autognome (a type of robot created typically by, well, gnomes). Her parents, Elyssa and Kireh, also frequently make appearances in these stories, as well as her son, Dave (another robotic construct made primarily from spare Clockwork Horror parts). The other party members are Miji, a Hadozee Monk--a monkey-like person with mystical martial arts skills, and Nylos, a Changeling Rogue who can communicate with the dead. Our other main allies are Lyania, our elven captain and oldest ally in our quest, and Soluna, the last survivor of the ancient Asrian people, who were eradicated eons ago by the Clockwork Horrors.
Into the Shroud is an upcoming D&D campaign I will be playing in. In it, the world has been enveloped by a mysterious veil of darkness; it corrupts and kills anything that it touches. The last bastion of civilization hunkers down in a crowded city, struggling to survive, a century after the world's greatest mage sacrificed his life to create a realm of safety.
Wish over Yonder is an ongoing DnDestiny campaign I am playing in; DnDestiny is a TTRPG based on combining D&D 5e with the world of Destiny (the video game franchise created by Bungie). The campaign, set many years before the events of modern Destiny, follows a group of outcasts as they find a clutch of Ahamkara eggs (magical, wish-granting, shape-shifting dragons).

The character I am currently playing, Aiash, is a Hive Knight specially created by the Hive's goddess of deception and trickery, Savathun. He initially infiltrated the party under the guise of Meta, a Guardian who had lost his Ghost, before eventually revealing his identity to his new companions, Ren (a Strand Hunter) and Valerie (a Void Warlock).

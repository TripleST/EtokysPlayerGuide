Welcome to the Etokys Player's Guide!

This supplement offers a variety of optional content for DMs and players alike to incorporate into their world. This is intended to be overall compatible with both pre- and post-2024 remaster rulesets. As such, some features may only work as intended for one particular version; these differences are noted within the features, which should hopefully make things a bit easier to follow! You may also find some lore here about the world of Etokys; feel free to use it in your own games if you wish, or derive from it and spin your own stories! Etokys is a unique fantasy world developed for my own games, and it has been developed specifically to allow for easy incorporation of any and all of the content found here.

Please note that all content here is homebrewed content made by me; this is unofficial and fanmade, so it may not be available at every table. Further, I have not used AI to generate ideas or game mechanics, and I never will. All content displayed here is proudly human-made.

If you would like to view the content as a whole, you may find the pdf here (link coming soon!). Otherwise, you can navigate the various sections in smaller pieces down below.

Lastly, I would just like to say thank you to anyone reading this, and especially for those who have supported me through the development of this supplement. Your excitement and support helped keep me motivated and brewing. While I cannot guarantee I will continue creating content for D&D in the future, if you would like to support me, whether as compensation for all the hours put into this or just to help me keep making fun stuff in the future, you can donate to me here.

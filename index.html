Etokys Player's Guide

Welcome to the Etokys Player's Guide!

This supplement offers a variety of optional content for DMs and players alike to incorporate into their world. This is intended to be overall compatible with both pre- and post-2024 remaster rulesets. As such, some features may only work as intended for one particular version; these differences are noted within the features, which should hopefully make things a bit easier to follow! You may also find some lore here about the world of Etokys; feel free to use it in your own games if you wish, or derive from it and spin your own stories! Etokys is a unique fantasy world developed for my own games, and it has been developed specifically to allow for easy incorporation of any and all of the content found here.

Please note that all content here is homebrewed content made by me; this is unofficial and fanmade, so it may not be available at every table.

If you would like to view the content as a whole, you may find the pdf here (link coming soon!). Otherwise, you can navigate the various sections in smaller pieces down below.

This is just a soft release; while most content is finished, there are a handful of things that will be added soon, in the second content drop.

The following content will be added in the full release:
  • A new race/species option;
  • The Magnus class and all 11 of its subclasses;
  • A new subclass for Artificer, Bard, Barbarian, Cleric, Fighter, Monk, Ranger, Sorcerer, and Warlock;
  • 10 new infusions for the Artificer class;
  • A new aquatic monster that can be used alongside the expanded familiar rules;
  • Information about the Integration system, a set of rules a DM can use that allow for easy creation of higher-powered characters;
  • Advice and tables for one way DMs can run an Adventurer's Guild for players to interact with;
  • Rules and tables for ways to implement weather into a game, and how different weather conditions might impact mechanics;
  • A guide to the basic lore of the world of Etokys, for those running games in that world or just looking for inspiration for their own characters or settings;
  • And possibly even more! (I may or may not add more. It's a problem.)

Hotfix implemented! (09/27/2025)

Howdy y'all! While taking a look through some of the released content, I noticed a few issues that needed addressing, so things might not be the same as you remember them!
  • Arcane Bulwark's missing material component has been added.
  • Spontaneity Bard can no longer upcast beyond 9th level, as was always the intention.
  • Draconic Warlock spells no longer count against your known spells, which was always the intended functionality.
  • Cursed Clerics had some leftover text from its brainstorming days, somehow; that's now been removed.
  • The kitsune stat block was missing a feature or two I'd somehow neglected to add; I also took the chance to tone down its damage a little, as it seemed a little higher than it should be.
